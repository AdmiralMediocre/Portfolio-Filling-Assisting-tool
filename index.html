Welcome to the Clinical Portfolio Manager. This web application is designed to help medical students digitally fill out their clinical attachment logbooks (Portfolio) without needing physical paper.

You can type your patient cases directly into the form, save them to your browser for later editing, and download a perfectly formatted PDF ready for submission.

üìù Instructions

  • Auto-Load: The system will automatically load the standard template (`Template.pdf`).
  • Fill Data: Use the sidebar on the left to enter patient details, diagnosis, and reflections.
  • Khmer Support: The tool fully supports typing in Khmer (Unicode).
  • Save Work: Click "Save Data" to store your progress in your browser. You can close the tab and come back later.
  • Manage Files: Use the "View Saved Files" button to see your history, edit old cases, or clone them.
  • Export: When finished, click "Download PDF" to generate your final document.

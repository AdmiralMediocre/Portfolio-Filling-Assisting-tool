Welcome to the digital era of clinical documentation. This tool is designed to replace handwritten logbooks with a clean, digital interface that overlays your text directly onto the standard PDF template.

Key Features:

  • Offline Capable: Works 100% without internet after the first load.
  • Auto-Save Database: Your cases are saved in your browser history. You can close the tab and come back later.
  • Bulk Export: Select multiple cases and export them into a single PDF logbook.
  • Khmer Support: Full support for Unicode Khmer fonts (Battambang).

üìù How to Use

1. Filling the Form

On the left sidebar, enter the patient details, diagnosis, and your reflection. The text will automatically appear on the PDF preview on the right.

2. Saving Data

Click the üíæ Save Data button. This saves the entry to your local "Saved Files" list. Always save before exporting!

3. Managing Files

Click üìÇ View Saved Files to see your history. From here you can:

  • Edit: Re-open an old case to make changes.
  • Clone: Duplicate a case (useful if patient details are similar).
  • Delete: Remove unwanted entries.

4. Exporting to PDF

You have two options:

  • Single Export: Click "Download PDF" in the editor to download the current page.
  • Bulk Export (Recommended): Go to the "Saved Files" list, tick the checkboxes of the cases you want, and click ‚¨áÔ∏è Export Selected to PDF. This creates one file containing all selected cases.

üíæ Data Privacy & Storage

1. Data is stored on YOUR Device: This tool does not send data to any server. Your patient data lives inside your web browser's "Local Storage".

2. Do NOT Clear Cache: If you "Clear Browser History/Cache," you will lose your saved data. Please export your PDFs regularly as a backup.

3. Device Specific: Data saved on your computer will not appear on your phone, and vice-versa.
